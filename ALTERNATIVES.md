# Do You Need RPFM? Alternatives Explained

## What is RPFM?

RPFM (Rusted PackFile Manager) is a tool that **extracts** Total War `.pack` files into readable formats (TSV, XML, text files, etc.).

**Think of it like unzipping a ZIP file** - pack files are compressed archives, and RPFM unpacks them.
